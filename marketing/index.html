How it works

Three steps to a tidy bookkeeping month.

No imports, no spreadsheets, no clicking through endless screens. Connect once, then chat.

1

Sign in & connect

Log in with Google, then attach your storages — e-conomic and your inbox — in a couple of clicks.

2

Just ask

“Find receipts for my April expenses.” The agent reads your books, hunts down each receipt, and checks it.

3

Confirm & done

What it does

More than a chatbot — it actually does the work.

🔎

Finds missing receipts

Scans e-conomic for vouchers without documentation and matches each to the right receipt in your inbox.

✅

Checks before it books

It reads every receipt — amount, date, supplier, VAT — and verifies the match before anything touches your books.

📎

Files it for you

Attaches the receipt to the correct voucher in e-conomic. Drafts and booked vouchers alike.

🧾

Creates receipts

No receipt for that cash expense? It generates a clean bilag PDF from what you tell it, ready to attach.

📤

Drop files in chat

Snap a photo of a till slip or drag a PDF straight into the conversation — it takes it from there.

🇩🇰

Speaks your language

The app is in English, but write in Danish and the agent answers in Danish. Skriv bare løs.
